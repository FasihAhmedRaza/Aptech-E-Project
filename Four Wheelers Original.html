Car Insurance Comparison Sites with
Estimated Quotes.

Unlike Insurify and Compare, some comparison sites only provide estimated quotes,
based on aggregate information from your local division of insurance. Auto insurance rates
from these websites are based on what other drivers in your ZIP code with similar histories
are paying for insurance, rather than real-time quotes from insurance companies. That
means that quotes for specific coverage options for specific drivers––a policy for a Texas
driver seeking property damage protection after an incident of vandalism, say, or a driver
seeking accident forgiveness and bodily injury coverage after being in a past at-fault
accident––won’t be as accurate.

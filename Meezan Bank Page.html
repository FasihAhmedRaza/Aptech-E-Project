Takaful Coverage

Peace of Mind in Times of Adversity

Accidental Death and Permanent

To provide support to our customers in times of adversity, Meezan Bank provides Accidental Death and Permanent Disability*and ATM
Cash Withdrawal** Takaful coverage (Islamic Insurance) of up to 1 million to all account holders maintaining an average monthly
Balance of Rs.10,000/-or more.

This coverage is provided FREE of cost and the Takaful claim amount is based on the average balance maintained in your account
during the month preceding the date of the incident.

Accidental Death and Permanent Disability Takaful is offered to account holders of up to 60 years.
**ATM Cash withdrawal Takaful is offered to all deposit account holders irrespective of the customer's age against snatching within 30
minutes of the withdrawal or 1.5 km radius (whichever comes earlier).

This is a free service for all Chequing account hoolders maintaining monthly average balance of Rs. 10,000/-or above.

All new and existing accounts, (indivisual,Joint, Rupee or Foreign Currency chequing accounts, both Current and Mudarabah
based savings deposits), whether dormant or active, shall be automatically enrolled in the scheme.

In addition to indivisual and joint, sole proprietorships account are eligible for covering this scheme;however corporate
accounts, partnership firms and other account types are exculded from this scheme.

This scheme only applies to the balances of the chequing accounts and does not apply to the Certificates of Islamic Investments(COlls).

Accounts with last month average balance of less than PKR 10,000-are exculded from Takaful cover of Accidental Death and
Permanent Disability. Customers aged 60 or above are exculded from Takaful cover of Accidental Death and Permanent
Disability but eligible for the ATM cash withdrawal Takaful.

In case of joint accounts, only single coverage will be offered.
